About

Meet Prabhleen
Arora

I'm Prabhleen Arora — a Senior Psychologist with over 5 years of experience spanning clinical practice, psychometric assessment, and institutional mental health operations. I hold a Master's in Clinical Psychology from Lovely Professional University, where I graduated as University Topper and Gold Medallist, and I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Psychology.

My work spans the full spectrum of human experience: from executive burnout and career transitions to senior wellness, LGBTQIA+ affirmative therapy, crisis intervention, and corporate EAP programs. I have delivered 3,000+ counseling sessions and designed mental health frameworks that have reached thousands of individuals across universities, organizations, and remote populations.

I believe great therapy is both art and science — rooted in evidence, powered by empathy, and built around you. Whether you're a driven professional seeking clarity, a senior navigating life's transitions, or an organization investing in your people — I am here, every Sunday.
